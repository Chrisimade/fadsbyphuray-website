Our Policies

Shipping Policy

We deliver across Lagos and all Nigerian states. Island (Lekki, Ajah, VI, Ikoyi): ₦7,000. Mainland Lagos (Epe, Yaba, Ogba, Berger, Surulere, Ikeja): ₦6,000. Other parts of Lagos (Agege, Ketu, Oshodi, Ikorodu etc): ₦5,000. Other Nigerian States: ₦8,500 flat fee. Orders over ₦50,000 qualify for free shipping within Lagos.

Return Policy

We offer a 5-day return period from the date of delivery. Items must be returned in exactly the same condition as received — unworn, unwashed, with all original tags attached and no damage. Contact us within 5 days of receiving your order to initiate a return. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after inspection.
